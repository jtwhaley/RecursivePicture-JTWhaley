The list of Methdods and what they do:

Blend() - Takes the regular show image and then puts the cartoon network logo over it, sharing it where they are both at 50% ocupacity

deepFry() - Averages the 8 pixels around it and adds random values to them. Goes along with a meme format (Google deepfried memes for more info)
	    Adds a shredded shrek face over bensons face.

superMirror() - Mirros the page verticle and horizontaly, to get a quadruple frame on each quadrant of the picture

SmallerRecursivly(int,int,int,Picture) - takes an image of given size and makes it continuasly smaller fading into the top left corner

superGreen() - Makes the green value maxed out for a green tint over the whole image
